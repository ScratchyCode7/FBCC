About Our Church

FBCC is founded on 1990. Over the years, the Lord has proven Himself faithful in this church which started in a children ministry (Daily Vacation Bible School) on a small garage of a member with a few number of attendees.

The ministry continues to grow and more souls were won to Christ by God’s grace in this church. Up to date, the Lord has blessed FBCC with 9 outreaches and more to come. FBCC is also blessed to have almost 20 pastors and workers who labor and disciple more people to Christ. The fruit of the pastors’ hardwork is best seen to the growing number and life of the brethren who are actively participating in different church ministries.

As the Lord continue to bless us with strength, wisdom and provisions, FBCC will continue to do its mission: “To Know Christ and To Make Christ Known”.
